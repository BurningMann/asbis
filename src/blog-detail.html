X Sales Conference

ASBIS celebrated the 25th anniversary of the founding of the company in Belarus and held the X Sales Conference, at which new products and development directions were presented.

The event took place in the Green Park hotel complex, not far from Minsk, and for several days united all employees of the company. Speaking at the opening of the conference, the vice-president of ASBIS, Andrey Kostevich, spoke about the results of the group's work and the future development of the business, including the introduction of new products to the market, response to new technological challenges of the market.

Among Asbis' own innovations and software solutions is the ioT platform, developed under the Perenio brand.
The heads of internal divisions of ASIS also spoke at the conference. They talked about new products from Prestigio and Canyon, key projects and digital business transformation. Many new products and features await Asbis partners and consumers, and will be announced as they become ready to go to market.

Also at the conference were external speakers, representatives of the vendor Microsoft, who spoke about the development of Windows 10.
We touched on legislative developments in the world, in particular the topic of GDPR, personal data protection.
Have learned the skill of "Elevator Pitch" presentation.
In parallel with the conference, sports events were held for employees of non-profit divisions.

This event turned out to be large-scale. And judging by the feedback from the participants, the level of the X Sales Conference met their expectations and the holiday, of course, was a success. Happy birthday, ASBIS BY.

Today the ASBIS group of companies holds leading positions in several technological areas. ASBIS successfully develops new technologies, takes into account modern legal and consumer requirements. All this is the merit of the large ASBIS team, which makes the world a better place every day.
